## Brand Voice
- Direct, honest, no fluff
- UK English spelling throughout (colour, optimise, recognised, licence)
- Professional but not corporate — write like a knowledgeable colleague
- Never use: "genuinely", "exciting", "game-changing", "revolutionary", "cutting-edge"
- Never use: "In today's digital landscape", "Let's dive in", "In conclusion"
- Short sentences. Short paragraphs. Never more than 3 sentences per paragraph.
- Always use £GBP, never $USD
