## Scoring Criteria

Each page is scored 0–6 against these binary (0 or 1) criteria:

| # | Criterion | Pass (1) | Fail (0) |
|---|-----------|----------|----------|
| C1 | **QUESTION_CLARITY** | Page answers a single, clearly identifiable question | Ambiguous or multi-topic |
| C2 | **H1_IS_QUESTION** | H1 phrased as a question or direct answer to one | Generic title or statement |
| C3 | **ANSWER_FIRST** | Direct answer appears within the first 50 words | Narrative, marketing copy, or preamble first |
| C4 | **FAQ_SCHEMA** | Page renders `FAQPage` or `Question` JSON-LD | No FAQ structured data |
| C5 | **INDEXABLE** | No `noindex` meta tag; page is crawlable | `noindex` present or blocked |
| C6 | **INTERNAL_LINKS_IN** | ≥ 2 distinct files link to this page | 0–1 inbound internal links |

### Priority Buckets

| Priority | Rule |
|----------|------|
| **HIGH** | Score 0–2 AND page is commercial (pricing, vertical landing, comparison, lead-gen) |
| **MEDIUM** | Score 3–4, OR score 0–2 on non-commercial pages with legitimate purpose |
| **LOW** | Score 5–6 |
| **KILL** | No clear question AND low traffic intent, OR functional page that should not be indexed |

## Template-Level Observations

### Strengths
- **Blog posts are exemplary.** 14 of 23 editorial posts score 6/6. The blog template enforces `FAQPage` JSON-LD, `BlogPosting` schema, `BreadcrumbList`, canonical URLs, and Open Graph metadata by default.
- **City landing pages score 6/6.** The `CityVerticalLandingPage` component uses question-phrased H1s ("Is AI Recommending [Profession] in [City]?") and includes FAQ schema — the best AEO template on the site.
- **Supplier directory pages are strong (5/6).** Category and location pages include `FAQPage` schema, conditional indexing (≥3 vendors), and structured data. Only the H1 format (statement instead of question) prevents a perfect score.
- **FAQ schema coverage is good.** 72% of pages (82/114) have `FAQPage` JSON-LD.

### Weaknesses
- **H1s are the #1 issue.** 48 pages use statement-format H1s. Template-level changes to the city report, supplier, and tools templates would fix 42 of them in 3 edits.
- **Orphan pages.** 12 pages have insufficient inbound internal links. Most are high-value pages (comparison, tools, resources) that should be cross-linked from the blog and vertical landing pages.
- **Functional pages are indexed.** 5 pages (review, vendor-claim, auth pages) serve no AEO purpose but are crawlable.
- **Tools section needs FAQ schema.** All 4 tool pages lack `FAQPage` JSON-LD despite having question-worthy content.
